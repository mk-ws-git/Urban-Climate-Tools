## Overview

The Forest Carbon Calculator from The Nature Conservancy helps landowners, planners, and researchers estimate carbon storage and sequestration potential in forest projects. This tool supports natural climate solutions by quantifying the climate benefits of forest conservation and reforestation.

### Key Features

- **Carbon Stock Estimation**: Calculate current carbon stored in forests
- **Sequestration Projections**: Model future carbon capture potential
- **Regional Factors**: Location-specific growth and carbon rates
- **Scenario Comparison**: Compare different management approaches
- **Report Generation**: Create documentation for carbon projects

## Use Cases

- Forest conservation project planning
- Reforestation and afforestation assessment
- Carbon offset project development
- Natural climate solutions analysis
- Land management decision support

## Getting Started

1. Visit [The Nature Conservancy](https://www.nature.org)
2. Navigate to the carbon calculator tools
3. Enter your project location and forest type
4. Review carbon estimates and projections
