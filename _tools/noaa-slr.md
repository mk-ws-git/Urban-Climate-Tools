## Overview

The NOAA Sea Level Rise Viewer is a powerful web-based tool that helps visualize community-level impacts from coastal flooding and sea level rise. The tool provides easy access to coastal flooding data and projections.

### Key Features

- **Sea Level Rise Scenarios**: Visualize impacts from 1-10 feet of sea level rise
- **Flood Frequency**: View how often areas may flood in the future
- **Marsh Migration**: Understand how coastal wetlands may respond to rising seas
- **Socioeconomic Impacts**: Analyze population and infrastructure at risk
- **Historical Hurricane Tracks**: View past storm surge impacts

## Use Cases

- Coastal resilience planning
- Emergency management and evacuation planning
- Real estate and insurance risk assessment
- Community education and outreach
- Infrastructure investment decisions

## Getting Started

1. Visit [NOAA Sea Level Rise Viewer](https://www.vdata.noaa.gov/slr)
2. Navigate to your area of interest
3. Select a sea level rise scenario
4. Explore impacts on different layers
