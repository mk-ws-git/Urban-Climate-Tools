## Overview

Open Climate Fix is a non-profit product lab building open-source tools to accelerate decarbonisation. Their flagship Solar PV Nowcasting model uses satellite cloud imagery and machine learning to predict solar generation 1–8 hours ahead, helping grid operators reduce reliance on fossil fuel backup capacity.

## Key Features

- **Nowcasting**: Accurate 0–4 hour solar PV forecasts using Meteosat satellite data
- **Open-source**: All code, models and training data published on GitHub under permissive licences
- **Grid integration**: Deployed in partnership with National Grid ESO to reduce carbon intensity of the UK grid
- **Research community**: Active contributor community and published research

## Who Is It For?

Data scientists, energy researchers, grid operators and smart-city teams wanting to integrate renewable energy forecasting into urban energy management systems.

## Getting Started

Explore the [Open Climate Fix GitHub organisation](https://github.com/openclimatefix) for repositories and documentation. The team also publishes notebooks and tutorials for getting started with the datasets.
