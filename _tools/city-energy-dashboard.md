## Overview

The City Energy Project Dashboard is a collaborative initiative between NRDC and the Institute for Market Transformation that helps cities track, benchmark, and reduce energy consumption in buildings. The platform provides data-driven insights for municipal energy management.

### Key Features

- **Energy Benchmarking**: Compare building performance across portfolios
- **Emissions Tracking**: Monitor greenhouse gas emissions over time
- **Policy Analysis**: Evaluate the impact of energy efficiency policies
- **Peer Comparison**: Compare your city to similar municipalities
- **Progress Reporting**: Generate reports for stakeholders and the public

## Use Cases

- Municipal energy benchmarking programs
- Climate action plan tracking
- Building efficiency policy development
- Public reporting and transparency
- Investment prioritization for retrofits

## Getting Started

1. Visit [City Energy Project](https://www.nrdc.org/citiesprogram)
2. Review the city participation requirements
3. Begin data collection for your municipality
4. Connect with the support team for implementation help
