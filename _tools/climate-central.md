## Overview

Climate Central is a leading online resource for climate science reporting and analysis. Their interactive tools help communities understand climate risks and inform adaptation planning.

### Key Features

- **Climate Risk Dashboard**: Visual representation of local climate risks including extreme heat, precipitation, flooding, and sea level rise
- **Sea Level Rise Viewer**: Interactive maps showing potential flooding impacts at different elevation levels
- **Extreme Weather Analysis**: Historical trends and projections for extreme weather events
- **Customizable Reports**: Generate tailored climate risk assessments for specific locations
- **Data Visualization**: Professional maps and charts suitable for presentations and reports

## Use Cases

- Municipal climate adaptation planning
- Community engagement and education
- Climate risk communication for stakeholders
- Evidence-based decision making for urban planning
- Insurance and real estate risk assessment

## Getting Started

1. Visit [Climate Central](https://www.climatecentral.org)
2. Navigate to the tools section
3. Select your location of interest
4. Explore interactive maps and data visualizations
5. Generate and export reports
