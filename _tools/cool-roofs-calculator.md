## Overview

The Cool Roofs Calculator from Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory helps building owners and designers estimate the energy savings and cooling benefits of cool roofing materials. The tool supports informed decisions about roofing investments that can reduce urban heat island effects.

### Key Features

- **Energy Savings Calculator**: Estimate annual energy cost savings
- **Material Comparison**: Compare different roofing products
- **Location-Based Analysis**: Results tailored to your climate zone
- **Payback Period**: Calculate return on investment
- **Environmental Benefits**: Quantify carbon reduction

## Use Cases

- Building retrofit planning
- New construction specifications
- Cool roof policy development
- Utility rebate program design
- Urban heat island mitigation planning

## Getting Started

1. Visit [Cool Roofs Calculator](https://coolroofs.lbl.gov)
2. Enter your building information
3. Select current and proposed roofing materials
4. Review savings estimates and recommendations
