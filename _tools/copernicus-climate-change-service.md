## Overview

The Copernicus Climate Change Service (C3S) is the European Union's official climate intelligence service, operated by ECMWF. It provides free, open access to high-quality climate data, projections and indicators that cities can use to understand current and future climate risks.

## Key Features

- **ERA5 Reanalysis**: Hourly estimates of climate variables from 1940 to present at ~31 km resolution globally
- **Climate projections**: CMIP6-based multi-model ensembles with RCP/SSP scenarios to 2100
- **Climate indicators**: Pre-computed urban-relevant metrics (cooling degree days, heat wave frequency, extreme precipitation)
- **Climate Data Store (CDS)**: API access and an interactive toolbox for custom data exploration

## Who Is It For?

Climate scientists, municipal planners and researchers who need reliable baseline and future climate data for vulnerability assessments, infrastructure design or policy analysis.

## Getting Started

Register for free at the [Copernicus Climate Data Store](https://cds.climate.copernicus.eu) to access datasets and the Python/R client library. The CDS Toolbox allows users to process data directly in the browser without downloading large files.
