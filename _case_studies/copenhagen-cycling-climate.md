## Challenge

Copenhagen needed to achieve carbon neutrality while improving transportation efficiency and urban livability. The city faced challenges in reducing fossil fuel dependence and adapting to climate change.

### The Solution

Copenhagen implemented comprehensive sustainable transport and energy strategies, focusing on cycling infrastructure, renewable energy, and district heating systems.

## Implementation

- **Cycling Infrastructure**: Extensive bike lanes and facilities making cycling the preferred transport mode
- **Renewable Energy**: Wind and solar power integration with smart grid systems
- **District Heating**: Waste heat recovery from industry and data centers

## Results

Copenhagen has achieved remarkable sustainability outcomes, becoming a global model for carbon-neutral urban development and sustainable transportation.
