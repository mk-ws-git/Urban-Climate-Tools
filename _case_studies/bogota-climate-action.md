## Challenge

Bogotá faces significant climate vulnerabilities including water scarcity, air pollution, and climate impacts exacerbated by poverty and inequality. The city needed to address climate adaptation while reducing social inequities.

### The Solution

Bogotá integrated climate action with social inclusion programs, creating green corridors, expanding public transit, and developing community-based adaptation strategies.

## Implementation

- **Green Corridors**: 50km of connected green spaces for mobility and climate adaptation
- **Transit Expansion**: 150 new bus routes to reduce emissions and improve access
- **Community Programs**: Training and employment in green infrastructure projects

## Results

The integrated approach has improved environmental conditions while creating economic opportunities and reducing social inequalities.
