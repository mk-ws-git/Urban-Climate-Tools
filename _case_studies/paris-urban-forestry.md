## Challenge

Paris faces urban heat island effects, poor air quality, and biodiversity loss in its dense urban environment. The city needed to rapidly expand green spaces while maintaining urban functionality.

### The Solution

Paris launched an ambitious urban forestry program to plant 170,000 trees across the city, focusing on heat-vulnerable areas and biodiversity enhancement.

## Implementation

- **Strategic Planting**: Trees selected for heat reduction, air quality improvement, and biodiversity
- **Maintenance Programs**: Community involvement in tree care and monitoring
- **Integrated Planning**: Coordination with existing green space management

## Results

The program has significantly improved urban climate conditions, enhanced biodiversity, and created a more livable city environment.
