## Challenge

Rotterdam, located below sea level, faces significant flood risk from both river flooding and sea level rise. The city needed innovative approaches to water management that integrate climate adaptation with urban development.

### The Solution

Rotterdam pioneered "water-adaptive" urban design, creating public spaces that function as both recreational areas and flood protection systems, while incorporating circular economy principles.

## Implementation

- **Water-Adaptive Design**: Public spaces that transform during flood events
- **Circular Economy Integration**: Using recycled materials and renewable energy in infrastructure
- **District-Level Planning**: Comprehensive resilience strategies for all 89 city districts

## Results

Rotterdam has become a global model for climate adaptation, successfully managing flood risk while creating attractive, sustainable urban environments.
