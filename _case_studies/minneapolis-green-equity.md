## Challenge

Minneapolis faces significant urban heat islands and stormwater flooding, particularly in low-income communities. The city needed to address both climate resilience and environmental justice through equitable green infrastructure deployment.

### The Solution

Minneapolis implemented a comprehensive green infrastructure program that prioritizes underserved neighborhoods, combining stormwater management with urban heat reduction and community economic development.

## Implementation

- **Equity-Focused Planning**: Used data analysis to identify neighborhoods with highest vulnerability and lowest green space access
- **Community Engagement**: Involved residents in project design and maintenance through participatory planning
- **Integrated Systems**: Combined rain gardens, permeable pavements, and urban forestry for multiple benefits

## Results

The program has successfully reduced urban heat, improved stormwater management, and created green jobs while addressing historical inequities in environmental access.
