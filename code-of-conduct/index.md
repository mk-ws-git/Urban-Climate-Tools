            <h1 class="section__title">Code of Conduct</h1>
        </div>

        <p class="about-lead">We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone who participates in Urban Climate Tools.</p>

        <div class="about-section">
            <h2 class="about-section__title">Our Pledge</h2>
            <p class="about-section__body">In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.</p>
        </div>

        <div class="about-section">
            <h2 class="about-section__title">Our Standards</h2>
            <p class="about-section__body">Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment include:</p>
            <ul class="about-section__body" style="margin-left: 1.5rem; line-height: 2;">
                <li>Using welcoming and inclusive language</li>
                <li>Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences</li>
                <li>Gracefully accepting constructive criticism</li>
                <li>Focusing on what is best for the community</li>
                <li>Showing empathy towards other community members</li>
            </ul>
            <p class="about-section__body" style="margin-top: 1rem;">Examples of unacceptable behaviour include:</p>
            <ul class="about-section__body" style="margin-left: 1.5rem; line-height: 2;">
                <li>The use of sexualised language or imagery, and unwelcome sexual attention or advances</li>
                <li>Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks</li>
                <li>Public or private harassment</li>
                <li>Publishing others' private information without explicit permission</li>
                <li>Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting</li>
            </ul>
        </div>

        <div class="about-section">
            <h2 class="about-section__title">Enforcement</h2>
            <p class="about-section__body">Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported by contacting the project team at <a href="mailto:urbanclimatetools@gmail.com">urbanclimatetools@gmail.com</a>. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.</p>
        </div>

        <div class="about-section">
            <h2 class="about-section__title">Attribution</h2>
            <p class="about-section__body">This Code of Conduct is adapted from the <a href="https://www.contributor-covenant.org" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Contributor Covenant</a>, version 1.4.</p>
            <p class="about-section__body">
                View the Code of Conduct on
                <a href="https://github.com/mk-ws-git/Urban-Climate-Tools/blob/main/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md">GitHub</a>.
            </p>
        </div>
