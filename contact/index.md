    <h2 class="about-section__heading">Partner With Us</h2>
    <p class="about-section__body">Are you a city authority, research institution, NGO, or climate tech organisation working on urban resilience? We are interested in collaborating in the following ways:</p>

    <div class="partner-ways">
      <div class="partner-way">
        <h4 class="partner-way__title">Feature Your City</h4>
        <p class="partner-way__body">We can work with you to document your city's climate resilience work as a peer-reviewed case study on this platform — giving your work global visibility and providing a model for other cities.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="partner-way">
        <h4 class="partner-way__title">Research Collaboration</h4>
        <p class="partner-way__body">We welcome academic partnerships to improve tool descriptions, add evidence-based ratings, and build comparative analyses of urban climate adaptation approaches.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="partner-way">
        <h4 class="partner-way__title">Institutional Data Sharing</h4>
        <p class="partner-way__body">If your organisation maintains datasets relevant to urban climate adaptation, we'd like to explore integration — particularly for the Climate Data Explorer dashboard.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="partner-way">
        <h4 class="partner-way__title">Translate &amp; Localise</h4>
        <p class="partner-way__body">Urban Climate Tools should be accessible regardless of language. If you can help translate the platform or tool descriptions into another language, we want to hear from you.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
